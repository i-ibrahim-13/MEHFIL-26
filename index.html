About MEHFIL

MEHFIL 2026 is the grandest cultural festival of the year, bringing together the most talented artists, performers, and creators from across the college. This three-day extravaganza celebrates the rich cultural diversity of our institution through music, dance, drama, art, and more.

Whether you're a performer, spectator, or volunteer, MEHFIL offers something for everyone. It's a platform to showcase your talents, make new connections, and create memories that last a lifetime.
